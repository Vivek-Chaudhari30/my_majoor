Welcome to Majoor 1.0 — your voice assistant for macOS, right in your menu bar.

This is our first App Store release, and we're excited to put it in your hands.

Majoor lives quietly in your menu bar and springs to life the moment you hold Ctrl+Option. Speak naturally — no wake word, no waiting. Release the keys and Majoor acts on what you said.

Here's what you can do in 1.0:

Open any app on your Mac by name, without touching your keyboard or mouse.

Search the web with a quick voice query and get results instantly in your browser.

Ask factual questions and get direct answers powered by AI.

Remember things across sessions. Tell Majoor to remember a fact and it will recall it the next time you ask.

Control your system hands-free — mute or unmute your volume, and adjust screen brightness, all by voice.

First-time setup is guided by a friendly onboarding flow that walks you through adding your OpenAI API key and taking Majoor for a spin.

We built Majoor to get out of your way and just work. We hope you love using it as much as we enjoyed building it.
